Download Game How to Play

5 Themed Levels

Jungle Dawn, Lava Cavern, Ice Peaks, Storm Ruins, Shadow Abyss — each with unique weather, obstacles, and increasing difficulty.

Online Multiplayer

Host a room, share a 6-character code with friends anywhere. Up to 4 players. Host approves joiners before the game starts.

Animal Enemies

Cats chase you, Dogs jump over obstacles, Dragons shoot fireballs. Shoot them with your gun or escape.

5 Playable Characters

Boy, Girl, Grandpa, Grandma, Officer — each with a unique look. Enter your name and pick your hero before playing.

Survival Mechanics

3 lives, HP bar, energy bar, ammo system. Collect hearts to restore health. Shield pickups for temporary invincibility.

Gun Combat

Press F or click to shoot. 12 bullets per life shown in the HUD. Gunshot sound, bullet trail effects, and kill scores.
